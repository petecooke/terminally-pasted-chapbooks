Regarded by many as The Godfather of Memphis Rap, DJ Spanish Fly tells his story in simple terms. As far as Bluff City hip-hop goes, there was no one before him. With all his dues paid and many careers inspired from his influence in the genre, there’s nothing left for him to do but kick back and have his fun. This nighttime variety show finds him opening up the hotlines to find answers to burning questions (120min)
