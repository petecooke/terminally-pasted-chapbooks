TERMINALLY PASTED HORSERACING

Blinkers Off

Jared Welch and Aaron Halterman - Featuring your resident Racing Dudes, Blinkers Off delivers weekly insights into the world of horse racing including race selections and previews, handicapping methods, exclusive stories, interviews, and more. (90min)

At The Races

Steve Byk - At the Races with Steve Byk SiriusXM radio show is your one stop place on the web & on the radio, for the latest in thoroughbred news, reports, tips, hints, & real horse action. (180min)

DRF TV

Race of the Day - DRF Handicappers discuss the day's most important or best betting race. Stakes Previews provide in-depth commentary and analysis that goes beyond the past performance lines. Viewers can expect coverage of all the major stakes races featuring DRF Handicappers Dan Illman, Mike Beer, David Aragona and many more. (15-20min)

The Owner's Box

Billy Koch and Michelle Yu - Focus on horse racing ownership, discussing the sport and the process of becoming a horse owner. (30min-ish)

JK + 1

Jonathon Kinchen - Provides an in-depth look at the people who shape the sport of horse racing, featuring interviews with professional horseplayers, Grade 1 winning owners, jockeys, trainers, bloodstock agents, and fellow media members. (50min-ish)

Players' Podcast

Peter Thomas Fornatale (PTF) and Jonathon Kinchen (JK) with Mikee P, Nick Tammaro, Dean Keppler, and others - Discussions on racing news, analysis of upcoming races, and commentary from industry professionals such as handicappers, trainers, owners, and jockeys. (30-45min)

TDN Writers' Room

Bill Finley, Zoe Cadman, and Randy Moss - Features a weekly racing discussion along with stories from the TDN Look and longer stories in the TDN which have been made into podcasts. (60min)

Rail Talk

Joe Bianca and Jon Green - latest coverage of racing's biggest events happening throughout the year. (45-90min)

American Gambler Book Club

Horseracing adjacentDavid Hill - Each week I invite friends and guests to discuss a gambling-themed book (however loosely-defined) and whatever else may come up. Writers, entertainers, bon vivants and other smart and interesting people help me figure out what all belongs in the canon of books about gamblers as I work on my own contribution to whatever that is. (60-80min)

35000 Watts

Just because...Michael Millard and Lisa Ray Girard - During the course of making the film, we met so many people with interesting stories to tell. We had so many conversations about music and culture and all the hilarious things that happen in a college radio station. In short, we discovered way more fun stuff than we could ever fit in a feature film. So, we started this podcast so we could continue to tell the story of college radio. (20-60min)
