Radio Station Wants

WWOZ - (new orleans, la) [ARCHIVE]

A community radio station currently operating out of the French Quarter in New Orleans. WWOZ's mission is to be the worldwide voice, archive, and flag-bearer of New Orleans culture and musical heritage.

KZMU - (moab, ut) [ARCHIVE]

Quirky radio show in beautiful Moab, UT. KZMU Community Radio 90.1 and 106.7 FM. Every third Friday 9-11pm. MST (GMT-6). Check out the playlists. Lots of instruments. Not too many computers. https://open.spotify.com/playlist/0C4kvAURLfi2wR6vfeTFjE

WESU - (middletown, ct) [ARCHIVE]

One of the oldest continually operating College Radio Stations in the nation, broadcasting alternative radio from the Heart of CT since 1939. Locally-produced music and talk shows hosted by students and community members run 4pm to 4am. The afternoon and late night mix includes a range of music including jazz, big band, reggae, hardcore, emo, metal, ambient, underground house, electronic music from southern Africa, Black and queer club music, punk, and more.

KAOS - (olympia, wa) [ARCHIVE]

KAOS 89.3 FM is a non-profit, non-commercial, college station that has invited the community to participate! KAOS is licensed to The Evergreen State College, Washington’s innovative four-year college, nationally acclaimed for its interdisciplinary studies in the liberal arts and sciences. KAOS promotes music from new artists, small record companies, and material that receives little (if any) airplay in the United States.

WMPG - (portland, me) [ARCHIVE]

WMPG is a community radio station broadcasting from Portland, Maine. It is located on Bedford Street at the University of Southern Maine Portland Campus. It is affiliated with the college, and a mix of USM students and volunteers from the greater Portland community produce all the music and local public affairs programs.

WZBC - (newton, ma) [ARCHIVE]

Devotion to experimental music with "no commercial potential" (aka NCP)

WHRB - (cambridge, ma) [ARCHIVE]

Harvard Radio's daily broadcast of Classical music, Jazz, and underground Rock explore a great repertoire of music left largely untouched by other commercial stations.

WMFO - (medford, ma) [some ARCHIVE]

Our dedication to the freeform ideology and “keeping it freeform” has and continues to provide the greatest freedom of expression for our DJs. From pop to rock, classical to hip hop, and talk to live bands

WNYU - (new york, ny) [no archive]

WNYU is NYU's radio station that is completely operated by NYU students.

WKCR - (new york, ny) [no archive]

WKCR exists to preserve and share music, the arts, and history with listeners in the New York metro area and beyond, curating programming that pushes boundaries while maintaining an eye to historical and artistic value, regardless of commercial significance.
